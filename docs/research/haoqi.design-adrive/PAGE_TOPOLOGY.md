# aDrive Project Page Topology

Source scope: `https://haoqi.design/adrive` only.

1. Fixed site chrome: top-left `HAOQI.DESIGN`, compact top-right controls, left-side vertical identity badge.
2. Independent article scroller: fills the viewport and owns the actual 11,034px scroll height.
3. Article header: title, date, divider, and project summary.
4. Teambition section: heading, paragraph, two 2251x1620/1621 images, closing paragraph.
5. ADS section: design-system introduction and architecture image.
6. Design principles: three principle subsections with four images.
7. ADS tooling: copy plus one tall tooling image.
8. Product overview: overview image, four feature explanations, and five additional long images.
9. Article footer: divider and outbound site links.

All article media is a single 832px desktop column. The site chrome is independent from the article scroll layer.
