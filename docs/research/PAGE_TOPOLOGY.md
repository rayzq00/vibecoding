# Design Tips Today Topology

Source: https://designtips.today/

- Root: white canvas, centered vertical stack, desktop padding `80px 40px`, 60px section gap.
- Intro: circular black portrait, two-line biography, then a 64px category rail.
- Category rail: click-driven. Articles, Projects, and About are local states; Notes opens `notes.designtips.today` in a new tab.
- Default state: About. A landscape photograph begins the content and is followed by a two-column information layout.
- About content: experience list, speaking cards, outbound links, hobbies, app list, and a small footer.
- Desktop content column: image and information area are constrained to `800px`; experience has a 493px left column and 247px right column with a 60px gap.
- Mobile: page becomes one narrow content column. The rail remains horizontally scrollable rather than wrapping.
